MULATA (CLASSIC CUBAN COCKTAIL)

1.5 ounces light rum 3/4 ounce dark creme de cacao 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, or to taste 1 cup ice cubes Combine all of the ingredients in a bar shaker, cover, amd shake well. Strain the mulata into a martini glass. this combination serves one. Steven Raichlen, Miami Spice
